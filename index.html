Empire of the Sun

Australian Electro Space Pop

Luke Steele and Nick Littlemore

  • 2007: Formed in Sydney as a collaboration between Luke Steele, former singer of "The Sleepy Jackson", and Nick Littlemore, mastermind behind "Pnau"

  • August 2008: Release of their Debut Single "Walking on a Dream", reaching No.10 in the Australian Singles Charts

  • September 2008: Release of their second Single "We are the people"

  • October 2008: Release of their Debut Album "Walking on a Dream", reaching No. 6 in the Australian Album Charts

  • June/September 2009: Releases of their Singles "Standing on the Shore" and "Without you"

  • September 2009: Live Debut at the Parklife Music Festival Australia

  • October 2009: Winning seven awards at the Australian ARIA Music Awards, including Album of the Year, Song of the Year and Best Group

  • April 2010: International Live Debut at the Vivo Latino Festival in Mexico City

  • October 2010: International Breakthrough with their single "We are the People" played in a Vodafone TV Spot, reaching No.1 in the German Singles Charts

  • June 2010 - October 2011: World Tour across Europe, USA, South America, Australia and Asia

  • April 2013: Release of their second album lead single "Alive", reaching No.1 in the US Dance Club Songs Charts (played e.g. in "Dumb and Dumber To" and "Fifa 14")

  • June 2013: Release of their second album "Ice on the Dune", reaching No. 3 in the Australian Album Charts

  • May 2013 - December 2015: "Ice on the Dune" Concert Tour around the World

  • January 2016: Their Debut Single "Walking on a Dream" reaches No.1 in the US Billboard Dance Charts, after used in a Honda Civic TV Commercial

  • October 2016: Release of their third album "Two Vines"
